
Inputs:
A & D = Move left / Move right
W = Jump

Space = Use Power

Powers::
Sonic = Become invincible while you spin
Tails = Float while you spin (Only activatable in the air)
Shadow = Runs fast (Only activatable on the ground)

Enemies cannot be killed and will destroy themselves when touched or after a long delay.
Only Sonic can touch enemies without dying (With Power)
